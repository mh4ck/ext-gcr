How to Request a Car?

Step 1: Launch the App Starting is as easy as launching our Gozo Car Rental app on your device. Simply tap on its icon on your home screen.

Step 2: Navigate to 'Request' form Once the app is open, look for the 'Request' option in the bottom tab menu, and tap on it to begin the process.

Step 3: Fill in the Form The form will ask for various details. You'll need to provide your prefered contact method, and the car specifications you're interested in. Take your time to fill out these fields accurately.

Step 4: Submit Your Request Review all the information you have entered to ensure it's correct, then hit the 'Submit' button. Your request will then be sent to our network of car rental companies on Gozo.

Step 5: Wait for Confirmation You will receive a confirmation message through the app or via your prefered contact method. One of our affiliated car rental companies will contact you to complete the process.

Please note: Handling times can vary depending on the car rental company's workload and operational hours. Rest assured, we're dedicated to providing you with the best service possible and will strive to meet your needs in a timely manner.

Remember, patience is key! You have successfully requested a car. Now, you just have to wait for confirmation. In the meantime, please feel free to explore the other features of our Gozo Car Rental App!
